About Me

Fresh Indie Developer | Creative Designer | Self-Taught Coder

Who I Am

Hey there! I'm Anik Paul, a 20-year-old indie game developer and graphic designer based in Assam, India. I'm a completely self-taught creator who believes in learning by doing and coding for the pure joy of it.

My journey into the world of digital creation began in 2021 when I started exploring graphic design and editing. What started as a curiosity quickly turned into a passion that fuels everything I do today.

What Inspires Me

I'm deeply inspired by puzzle-adventure games like Assassin's Creed Mirage and Japanese-themed action games like Shadow Fight 2 and Ninja Arashi 2. These games showed me how creativity, storytelling, and mechanics can create unforgettable experiences.

My favorite games are Ninja Arashi 2 and AC Mirage - they inspire me to create games that are both challenging and visually captivating.

My Dream & Goals

Right now, I'm focused on developing The Legend of Tenma, a 2D adventure game that combines my love for Japanese aesthetics with engaging puzzle mechanics. It's my most ambitious project yet, and I'm pouring my heart into it.

Beyond that, I want to keep creating games that challenge players, tell meaningful stories, and push the boundaries of indie game development. I do this because I love it - every line of code, every pixel, every design brings me joy.

My Philosophy

"I do and learn for fun."

That's my mantra. I believe the best creations come from pure passion and curiosity. Being self-taught has taught me that limitations are just opportunities to innovate. Every challenge is a chance to learn something new, and every project is an adventure.
